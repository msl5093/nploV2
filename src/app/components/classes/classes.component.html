Beginner Flow

Vinyasa means to link breath with movement. This flow yoga will focus on flowing though poses with focus on both strengthening and lengthening.

The practices as part of this class are considered introductory and thus are recommended for anyone open to exploring yoga. You will get a workout so it is also recommended you are comfortable with a moderate level of physical activity and endurance.

Gentle Yoga

This class will use props and chairs to gently move into poses and will be focused on alignment and finding ease in each pose.
