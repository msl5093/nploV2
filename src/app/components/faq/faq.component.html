What is you cancellation policy?

Please email or contact us through Facebook if you would like to know if a class is cancelled or otherwise not available.

It’s a safe bet that if there is any type of snow emergency or school closure in the greater Waynesboro area, that classes will be cancelled. This is for your safety as well as ours.

There are also times when we need to cancel classes due to family commitments. Please reach out via email, Facebook, or phone and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Where are you located?

We are located at 10 E Main Street Waynesboro, PA. Right on the square near Brio coffee.

What are your hours?

Classes are offered Tuesday and Thursday evenings as well as Saturday mornings. Specialty offerings vary. Check out our schedule page for detailed dates and times.

How much does a class cost?

Classes are $10 a visit.

What types of payment do you accept?

Cash, check, and all major credit cards are accepted.
